Introduction of Team 7, from leaf village

Kakashi Hatake, though looking cool and calm has an very unpleasent past. The sensei of Team 7, one of the most dangerous person in the shinobi world(also known as Kakashi of the sharingan). The only alive student of late hokage MInato Namikaze he had no friends except might Guy. Copy Ninja Kakashi has a very dominating presence.


After the fourth great ninja war against Madara Uchiha, he was appointed as the 6th hokage of hidden leaf. Leaving his contribution to the shinobi world, he is still one of the most fearfull person.

Naruto Uzumaki, the most stupd but th brightest in team 7. The jinjuriki of the NIne-tailed demon Kurama. Always isolated in the village due NIne-tailed demon, Naruto had a very lonely childhood. Both his parents died during the attack from nine tails while saving naruto.


After sealing Kaguya Otsutsuki at the end of Fourth Great Nnja War, he was hailed as a hero, and had a lot of friends. He comleted his ambition of becoming a hokage by successing this title from his sensei Kakashi Hatake.
